Strawberry Milkshake

This strawberry milkshake is thick, creamy, and everything that you would hope a milkshake would be!

Made with delicious strawberries, this classic dessert is sure to be loved by all!

ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Frozen Sliced Strawberries
  • Milk
  • Whipped Topping

Steps

  1. Add Ice Cream: Place the scoops of vanilla ice cream in a blender.
  2. Pour: Pour in the milk
  3. Combine: Add the sweetened strawberries.
  4. Blend: Blend on high until the ingredients are smooth and incorporated. Top with whipped topping, and enjoy!
